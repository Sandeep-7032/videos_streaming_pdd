Monitoring

Monitor your child's activity on Kids World, including the content they interact with, how long they spend on the platform, and their general behavior.

As a parent, it’s crucial to stay informed about your child's engagement with online content. Set up personalized activity reports and get instant notifications about any changes or irregularities in their usage.

Screen Time Setup

Set time limits to control how much time your child spends on Kids World. You can set daily or weekly limits to ensure your child gets enough time for other activities.

Kids World allows you to monitor their screen time usage, and you’ll get alerted when they reach their daily or weekly limits. You can also pause screen time access remotely if necessary.

Activity History

View a detailed history of your child’s activities, including the videos they’ve watched, games they’ve played, and the interactive content they’ve engaged with.

Reviewing activity history helps you understand your child’s preferences and ensures they are interacting with content that aligns with your expectations. This feature is vital for guiding your child's learning and entertainment choices.

Content Filtering

Enable content filtering features to make sure your child only accesses age-appropriate material. Block out content that may be unsuitable or harmful for young viewers.

Content filtering can be customized to block categories of content, specific keywords, or even specific creators. This helps you create a safe and enjoyable environment for your child on Kids World.

Privacy Settings

Take control of your child’s privacy by adjusting their account settings. Decide what information they can share, and control who can interact with them on the platform.

We take privacy seriously and offer various tools to ensure your child’s data is protected. From limiting social interactions to controlling data sharing, you can rest assured that your child's information remains secure.
